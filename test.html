About the Game

Sinbound is a dark fantasy trading card game set in the world of the Hellskein. Players harness the powers of the Seven Deadly Sins to battle using Thralls, rituals, and forbidden pacts.

Rulebook

Objective: Reduce your opponent's Leader to 0 HP.

Deck: 60 cards, with optional Sin Pact.

Turn Phases: Start → Main → End. Players gain 1 Sin Engine and produce Sin at the start of their turn.

Thralls: Cannot attack the turn they enter play unless specified.

Full rulebook coming soon.

Card Types

  • Thralls – Creatures that attack, block, and generate Sin.
  • Spells – One-time effects.
  • Rituals – Powerful effects requiring specific Sin.
  • Relics – Permanent ongoing effects.
  • Leaders – Central figures with abilities and HP.

Lore

In the beginning, N.−000 — the Seamless One — was sent by Heaven to observe the growing corruption of the Hellskein. When the Sins manifested as entities, he consumed and sealed them within himself, becoming a vessel of divine horror.

His twin, N.999 the Final Hour, represents Time itself and descended to chain his brother within the Pale Hollow after countless angelic legions failed to contain the wrath of space incarnate.
